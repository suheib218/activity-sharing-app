Overview

The Activity Sharing App is a user-friendly platform designed to facilitate spontaneous event planning among friends and acquaintances. Built using Flask and SQLite, this application empowers users to seamlessly create, share, and join various activities in real-time. With an intuitive interface and essential features, the app provides a vibrant community space for users to connect and engage in activities they love. The app focuses on enhancing social interaction by allowing users to manage their activities efficiently. From casual gatherings to organized events, users can find and participate in activities that match their interests and schedules. This flexibility makes the app a valuable tool for fostering connections and enriching social lives.

Goals

The goals of the Activity Sharing App are to enhance user engagement by providing a platform for spontaneous event planning and social interaction among friends and acquaintances. The app aims to deliver a user-centric design that allows for easy activity management while fostering community building and ensuring robust security measures for user data. Additionally, it seeks to implement a feedback mechanism to continuously improve user satisfaction and accommodate future enhancements.

Key Features

  • User Registration and Login: Users can register, log in, and view their profiles.
  • Create Activities: Users can create new activities with a name, description, date, and time.
  • Join Activities: Users can view upcoming activities and join any activity they find interesting.
  • View Activity Details: Each activity has a detailed page displaying all the relevant information.
  • Profile View: Users can view the profiles of other participants, including their joined activities.
  • Activity Management: Users can update or delete activities they have created.
  • Role-Based Access Control: Only the creator of an activity can modify or delete it.
